Executive Email

Executive Email automates the email proof reading for mass emails. When you want to send a mass email, you send it to executive email. Executive email will immediately send it to your executives. If any of the executive reply within a preset time (say 4 hours), the mass email won't be sent, and you will receive the corrections from executives. If none of the executives reply, the mass email will be sent out in 4 hours.
